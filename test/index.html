Yulia Protasova

Yulia Protasova is a Ukrainian composer and pianist based in Valencia and Barcelona, Spain. She composes music for film, television, video games, and other media. Winner of the SGAE Award for Best Original Soundtrack at the Sitges International Film Festival 2024.

De-Sastre

Short film | 2024 | Comedy - Fantasy
Directed by: Tommaso Mangiacotti, Marolyn Ávila, Constanza Melio, María Antonieta Fernández, Kuang Yi Lee
Music: Yulia Protasova

Daniela (27) has just moved into an old apartment and is ready to make it her new home. As the months go by, Daniela notices something strange happening: her clothes have cuts and holes in peculiar shapes. Her busy life is affected by these interventions until one day, behind the old clock, she discovers the culprit of this disaster: a monster with an old tailor shop hidden behind the walls, who has been using Daniela's clothes to make coats for all the animals in the neighborhood. Daniela must decide what to do about this tenant, who has transformed her clothes and her way of seeing life.

Cyberpunk Nightcore

Short film | 2024 | Virtual Production Experimental Film
Directed by: Guillem Beltrà
Music: Yulia Protasova

Step into a world of neon lights and shadows with this experimental short film, crafted using cutting-edge virtual production techniques. Set in a mesmerizing cyberpunk city, the film introduces a mysterious humanoid character with hybrid creature-like features and a touch of darkness. This solitary figure, shrouded in mystery, finally emerges from the shadows to perform their ritual dance under the city’s enigmatic glow.Open to interpretation, this artistic video serves as both a character introduction and a visual exploration of identity, solitude, and ritual in a futuristic, dystopian setting
