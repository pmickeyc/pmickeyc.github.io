Our meditation retreats

We run a bi-monthly retreat out of our beautiful facility on the coast of County Clare, Ireland. Each retreat last 7 days / 6 nights and offers a full scheulde of deep meditative practice and healing hatha yoga workshops.

Room and board are included in the package, as well as excursions to some breathtaking local landmarks.
Mediation practices will include mindfulness sessions, metta practice and workshops on pranayama - all skill levels are welcome. Sign up now.

Meditation for Beginners

Try this simple meditation right now:

Take a deep breath when the square is red.
Hold this breath in.
Slowly release when the square is blue.

Repeat this ten times to achieve an instant calming effect.
