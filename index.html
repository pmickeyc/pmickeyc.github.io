Yulia Protasova

Yulia Protasova is a Ukrainian composer and pianist based in Valencia and Barcelona, Spain. She composes music for film, television, video games, and other media. Winner of the SGAE Award for Best Original Soundtrack at the Sitges International Film Festival 2024. In the same year, Yulia became a member of the Barcelona Film Composer Collective, where she continues to collaborate on new projects.
