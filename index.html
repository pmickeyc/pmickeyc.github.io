Yulia Protasova

Hey, my name is Yulia Protasova. I am a Ukrainian composer and pianist based in Barcelona and Valencia, Spain, creating music for film, television, video games, and other media. I am a multiple award-winning composer for Best Original Soundtracks, and a member of the Barcelona Film Composer Collective, where I collaborate on innovative projects.

Becoming Ema

Documentary
Directed by: Patricia Drati
Produced by: Sidsel Lønvig Siersted (Good Company Pictures, Denmark), Marcel Pazman (Frame Film, Slovakia)
Country of production: Denmark, Slovakia
Music: Yulia Protasova
Website: playlabfilms.com/project/becoming-ema

While expecting her second child, Ema decides to leave behind modern city life and start anew in the countryside of Mallorca. She convinces her husband, Tomas, that away from the social dogmas of career, wealth, and success, they will be able to raise their children in a simple and sustainable way. A year after making this dream come true, the pandemic arrives, and the family is forced to reevaluate their choices.

Cirk

Documentary series
Directed by: Carlotta Napolitano, Alejandro Cabrera
Written by: Carlotta Napolitano, Alejandro Cabrera
Editing and direction: Carlotta Napolitano, Alejandro Cabrera
Produced by: Judit Porta, Joana Vallès, Anna M. Bofarull, Catarina Brites
Music: Gerard Pastor, Yulia Protasova

Cirk is a documentary series about the growing wave of contemporary circus in Catalonia, following some of the most relevant artists, institutions, and companies in the local scene. Across 13 episodes, it explores topics such as motherhood, accessibility, architecture, and artistic research within the circus world.
